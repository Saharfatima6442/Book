# Thank You and Acknowledgments

## Gratitude and Recognition

As we conclude this comprehensive guide on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, it is important to recognize the contributions of many individuals, organizations, and communities that have made this work possible and continue to advance the field of robotics.

### To the Robotics Community

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to the global robotics community, whose collective efforts have made the concepts in this book possible. The open-source nature of robotics development, exemplified by projects like ROS and ROS 2, has democratized access to sophisticated robotic tools and frameworks. Researchers, developers, and enthusiasts worldwide have contributed countless hours to advancing the state of the art in robotics.

### To the AI Research Community

The recent breakthroughs in artificial intelligence, particularly in machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing, have revolutionized robotics capabilities. The development of large language models, vision transformers, and reinforcement learning algorithms has opened new possibilities for intelligent robotic systems. We thank the AI researchers whose work forms the foundation of modern intelligent robotics.

### To Hardware Innovators

The advancement of humanoid robotics would not be possible without the continuous innovation in hardware. We acknowledge the engineers and companies developing more capable actuators, sensors, computing platforms, and robot bodies. Their work transforms theoretical concepts into practical realities.

### To Educational Institutions

Universities and research institutions worldwide have fostered environments where robotics research can flourish. The students, professors, and researchers in these institutions continue to push the boundaries of what is possible in humanoid robotics. Their work provides the theoretical foundation and practical insights that inform this book.

### To Industry Leaders

Companies developing humanoid robots and related technologies are turning research into real-world applications. Their investment in robotics development accelerates progress and brings sophisticated robotic systems closer to widespread deployment. We recognize their role in bridging the gap between research and practical application.

### To the Open Source Community

The robotics field has greatly benefited from open-source development. Projects like ROS, Gazebo, MoveIt, and countless others have provided accessible tools for robotics development. We thank the developers who have shared their work with the community, making advanced robotics accessible to more people around the world.

### To Beta Readers and Reviewers

We are grateful to the individuals who reviewed early drafts of this book and provided valuable feedback. Their insights helped improve the clarity, accuracy, and usefulness of the content. Any errors that remain are solely our responsibility.

### To Supporting Organizations

We acknowledge the funding agencies, research grants, and institutional support that enable robotics research and development. These organizations recognize the importance of robotics for the future and provide the resources necessary for continued progress.

### To Our Students and Colleagues

The questions, insights, and challenges posed by students and colleagues have shaped our understanding of how to present these complex topics. Their curiosity and engagement have been a constant source of inspiration and motivation.

### To the Future Generation

Finally, we thank the students and practitioners who will read this book and continue advancing the field of humanoid robotics. Your curiosity, creativity, and dedication will drive the next generation of breakthroughs in Physical AI and robotics. The future of robotics will be shaped by your contributions, and we are excited to see what you will achieve.

## Continuing the Journey Together

The field of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics is rapidly evolving, and this book represents a snapshot of current knowledge and capabilities. We encourage readers to contribute to the ongoing development of this field by:

- Sharing your implementations and improvements with the community
- Contributing to open-source robotics projects
- Pursuing research that addresses current limitations
- Developing applications that benefit society
- Educating others about robotics and AI

The future of humanoid robotics will be created by people like you, applying the knowledge and skills developed through books like this one. We are grateful that you have chosen to join us on this journey and look forward to seeing how you will contribute to the advancement of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

Thank you for your interest in this field and for your commitment to advancing the capabilities of intelligent robotic systems. The work you do today will shape the human-robot interaction of tomorrow.
