# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the complete guide on **Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics**. This comprehensive book covers everything you need to know about creating intelligent, autonomous humanoid robots that can interact naturally with humans and operate effectively in real-world environments.

This book is organized into 12 comprehensive chapters that progressively build your understanding of humanoid robotics:

- **Chapter 2**: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)

### Part II: Perception and Interaction

## About This Book

This book follows a progressive learning model that takes you from fundamental concepts to practical implementation. Each chapter builds upon previous knowledge, creating a comprehensive understanding of humanoid robotics systems.

The content aligns with the principles outlined in the Constitution for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics, emphasizing:
- Real-world application of AI knowledge to humanoid robots
- Integration of digital brain and physical body
- Natural human-robot interaction
- Safety and ethical considerations

## Target Audience

This book is designed for:
- Beginners to intermediate learners in AI and Robotics
- Software developers transitioning into robotics
- Students preparing for advanced humanoid robotics projects
- Engineers seeking hands-on experience with ROS 2, simulation, and embodied AI
