# Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI - Feature Specification

## Feature Overview
This feature involves creating the first substantive chapter of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics book. The chapter titled "Introduction to Physical AI" will establish foundational concepts and principles of physical artificial intelligence, which combines AI algorithms with physical systems like robots.

## Requirements
- Comprehensive coverage of Physical AI fundamental concepts
- Clear explanations of how AI differs when applied to physical systems
- Discussion of historical development and current state-of-the-art
- Introduction of key terminology and concepts
- Practical examples and applications of Physical AI
- Integration with the existing Docusaurus documentation structure
- Code examples demonstrating Physical AI concepts (Python-based)
- Appropriate depth for readers with intermediate knowledge of AI and robotics

## Scope
- Theoretical foundations of Physical AI
- Distinction between traditional AI and Physical AI
- Sensory-motor integration principles
- Embodied cognition concepts
- Physics-aware AI systems
- Hardware considerations for Physical AI
- Safety and ethical considerations in Physical AI
- Future directions and open challenges

## Out of Scope
- Detailed mathematical derivations (will be referenced)
- Deep dives into specific hardware implementations
- Comprehensive review of all existing Physical AI systems
- Advanced control theory mathematics

## Success Criteria
- Complete, well-structured chapter document with appropriate headings
- Clear, accessible explanations with diagrams where beneficial
- Functional code examples that demonstrate key concepts
- Consistent formatting with other chapters in the book
- Proper integration with Docusaurus navigation
- Technical accuracy as verified by domain knowledge
